Semantic Tags (meaning wale tags)
Ye HTML ko meaning dete hain, SEO friendly hote hain.

Non-Semantic Tags (meaning nahi dete)
Jaise: <div>, <span>
Sirf layout banane ka kaam krty hy.

Semantic Tags Explanation (Short):
1. <main>
Page ka main content yahan hota hai.
Header, footer isme nahi ate.

2. <section>
Page ka topic-based part.
Jaise: Features section, Services section.

3. <article>
Aisa content jo independently exist kar sakta ho.
Jaise: Blog post, news article, card.

4. <aside>
Side content — usually sidebar, ads, related links.
